There are three types of Bank Accounts :

1.Savings A/C
        -- Household
        -- Some restrictions(no. of trans-
           actions,cap on daily withdrawal,
           minimum balance maintenance)
        -- Low Interest
        -- Demand Deposit + Time Deposit

2.Current A/C
        -- Business Entities
        -- No restrictions
        -- No or negative interests(3.6%)
        -- Demand Deposit

3.Recurring/Fixed Deposits
        -- All Entities
        -- Some restrictions
        -- Relatively higher interest rates
           (6.7%)
        -- Time Deposits
